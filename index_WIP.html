Groups I Support

Here are the groups that I am currently a part of and continue to support:

People to People

People to People is a student ambassador program that allows students to explore many different countries and their culture. Students get to learn about cultures, ideas, and ways of life that could be very different from what they are used to in the U.S.

ATA

The American Taekwondo Association (ATA for short) is a global organization that teaches self-defense and self-discipline to people of all ages.

Personally, ATA has taught me a lot about myself and what I am capable of doing both physically and mentally. Additionally, I learned how to control my thoughts and my actions appropriately. Starting the program at a young age provided me with a great environment to grow as an individual while also giving me a home away from home where I could connect with people who shared a common interest.
